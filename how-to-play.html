How to Play Tic-Tac-Toe

Setup

  • Draw a 3x3 grid (three rows and three columns).
  • One player is "X", the other is "O".

Taking Turns

  1. Players take turns placing their symbol (X or O) in an empty square.
  2. X always goes first.
  3. Once a square is filled, it cannot be used again.

How to Win

  • The first player to get three of their symbols in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins.
  • If all squares are filled and no one has three in a row, the game is a draw.
