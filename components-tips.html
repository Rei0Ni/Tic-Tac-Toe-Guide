Game Components & Simple Tips

What You Need

  • A 3x3 grid (drawn on paper or a board)
  • Two players
  • Two symbols: X and O

Simple Tips

  1. Try to take the center square if you can.
  2. Block your opponent from getting three in a row.
  3. Look for ways to create two winning moves at once.
